Programme

The programme series has three workshops, all of them taking place in St. Luke's Campus, University of Exeter.
For the practical sessions A and B (introductions to ImageJ and QuPath respectively), the participants should bring their own laptop with the software previously installed. Should you have any problems installing the software, let us know prior to the event. Let us also know about any specific requirements. You can contact the organisers Dr. Pia Leete, Dr. James Crichton, and Dr. Nicolás Verschueren via email (just type their names in the university email). Below is an overview of the programme, the activities with an (L) next to the title are (L)ectures and those with (W), workshops.
