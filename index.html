Workshop on Image Analysis

Welcome the official website for the Image Analysis workshop. This workshop will be taking place at St Luke's Campus, University of Exeter, and it consists of three parts: Introduction to ImageJ (2/5), Basic Guide to QuPath (10/5), and a whole-day activity with two speakers and a questions and answers lunch meeting (16/5). In this website you can find an updated version of the programme, the materials for the different sessions and specific information about the event. Use the top bar to navigate through the different sections of the website

This is an in person event, free for staff and students of the University of Exeter. Registration is required.
