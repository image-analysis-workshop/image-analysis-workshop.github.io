The ROI Manager


When creating a figure from your images it is often important to be able to select only a portion of the image
  • Cropping images
  • Zooming images

      • It is important that the size and position of the selected region are known and recorded
      • This can help to preserve scales and sizes between different images

      This is one of the many uses of the ROI (region of interest) Manager
